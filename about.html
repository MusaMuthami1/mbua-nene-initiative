Our Journey

From humble beginnings to impacting thousands of lives across Kenya.

2020

Foundation

Mbua Nene Initiative was founded by a group of passionate individuals committed to addressing the challenges facing rural communities in Kenya.

2021

First School Project

Successfully built our first primary school in Kiambu County, providing education access to over 200 children in the community.

2022

Healthcare Expansion

Launched mobile health clinics program, bringing medical services to 15 remote communities across three counties.

2023

Water Initiative

Completed 25 water well projects, providing clean water access to over 5,000 community members.

2024

Sustainable Growth

Expanded to 45 communities with 15 active projects, transforming over 2,500 lives through our comprehensive programs.
