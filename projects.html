Kiambu Primary School

Education
400 Students Enrolled
8 Classrooms
25 Teachers
2023 Completed

Project Overview

The Kiambu Primary School project transformed education access for children in rural Kiambu County. This modern facility features 8 fully-equipped classrooms, a library, computer lab, and administrative offices.

Impact Achieved

  • 400 children now have access to quality primary education
  • 25 teachers employed, boosting local economy
  • 100% increase in school enrollment in the area
  • Digital literacy programs reach 200+ students
  • Community pride and engagement significantly improved

Community Feedback

"This school has given our children hope for a better future. We are so grateful to Mbua Nene Initiative for believing in our community." - Grace Mwangi, Parent and Community Leader

Mobile Health Clinics

Healthcare
3 Mobile Units
15 Communities Served
2,500 Patients Treated
12 Medical Staff

Project Overview

Our mobile health clinic program brings essential medical services directly to remote communities that lack access to healthcare facilities. Each clinic is equipped with modern medical equipment and staffed by qualified healthcare professionals.

Services Provided

  • Primary healthcare consultations
  • Maternal and child health services
  • Vaccination programs
  • Health education and disease prevention
  • Emergency medical care
  • Chronic disease management

Current Progress

We have successfully deployed 2 mobile clinics and are raising funds for the third unit. The response from communities has been overwhelmingly positive, with significant improvements in health outcomes.
